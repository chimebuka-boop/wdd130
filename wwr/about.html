History

Founded in 1998 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts, White Water Rafting Adventures began as a small local guide service on the Colorado River. Over the years, the company grew steadily by prioritizing safety, personalized experiences, and environmental stewardship. Today, we are proud to offer guided rafting trips across multiple renowned rivers, welcoming thousands of adventurers each season. Our commitment to blending thrilling adventure with education about river ecosystems has made us a trusted leader in sustainable outdoor recreation. With decades of experience, our team continues to innovate and expand, ensuring every journey delivers unforgettable memories while protecting the natural beauty we cherish.
